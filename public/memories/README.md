# Your photos go here

Drop 4 photos named `1.jpg`, `2.jpg`, `3.jpg`, `4.jpg` in this folder and they
appear automatically in the polaroid "memories" strips on the home and plan
screens. Until then, tasteful gradient placeholders show in their place.

Square-ish images look best (they're cropped to a square frame). To change the
captions, edit `DEFAULT` in `components/Memories.tsx`.
